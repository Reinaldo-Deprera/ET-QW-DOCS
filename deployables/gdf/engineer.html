Anti-Personnel Turret

The Anti-Personnel Turret automatically detects and engages enemy foot soldiers with its rapid fire rotary cannon, but will not engage vehicles. This deployable can only be placed by the Engineer class.





Anti-Vehicle Turret

The Anti-Vehicle Turret automatically detects and engages any enemy ground or air vehicles with a salvo of guided missiles, but will not engage infantry. This deployable can only be placed by the Engineer class.





Artillery Interceptor Turret

The Artillery Interceptor Turret automatically detects and engages incoming Strogg bombardment projectiles, but cannot defend against the Violator orbital strikes. This deployable can only be placed by the Engineer class.
