Anti-Personnel Turret

The Anti-Personnel Turret automatically detects and engages enemy foot soldiers with its twelve rapid-fire plasma cannons, but will not engage vehicles. This deployable can only be placed by the Constructor class.





Anti-Vehicle Turret

The Anti-Vehicle Turret automatically detects and engages any enemy ground or air vehicles with salvos of Strocket missiles. This deployable can only be placed by the Constructor class.





Artillery Interceptor Turret

The Artillery Interceptor Turret automatically engages and destroys incoming GDF Fire Support, but cannot defend against GDF Airstrikes. This deployable can only be placed by the Constructor class.
