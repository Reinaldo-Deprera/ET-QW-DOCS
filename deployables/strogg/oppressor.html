Rail Howitzer

The Rail Howitzer is a Strogg bombardment device deployed and directed by the Oppressor class, firing a salvo of ten shells. It is very effective against area targets.




Plasma Mortar

The Plasma Mortar is a Strogg Bombardment device deployed and directed by the Oppressor class. It lobs a six-round salvo of plasma blasts, and is very effective against armored targets. You can Lock On to ground or air vehicles to home in on them. Experienced Oppressors can Lock On to targets instantly.



Dark Matter Cannon

The Dark Matter Cannon is the heaviest bombardment device deployed and directed by the Oppressor class. It projects an enormous Bosonic Orb of colossal destructive power, best viewed from a considerable distance.
