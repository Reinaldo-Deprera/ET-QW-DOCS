Canyon

Location: Kings Canyon, Northern Territory, Australia

Background: The Strogg have taken over an abandoned fortified base to develop an improved Stroggifier. The GDF must fight their way down the canyon and destroy the Stroggifier before it can be used to produce new armies of Stroggtroppers.

Objectives:

  1. The first Canyon objective requires a GDF Engineer to construct a bridge         blocking a critical path to the Central Outpost.
  2. Any GDF unit must drive the MCP to the Central Outpost
  3. The third objective requires a GDF Covert Ops to hack the Strogg Shield         Generator
  4. The fourth objective is the Stroggifier located in the mountain bunker. GDF         Soldiers need to destroy it by planting an HE Charge on it.


Optional Objectives:

  1. Capture the Domed Building Spawn Point
  2. Capture the Ruined Building Spawn Point
  3. Capture the Hut Spawn Point
  4. Construct the Guard Tower
