Island

Location: Reservation Island, South Atlantic

Background: Moore Observatory is the only surviving facility which still holds data on the appearance of the first Strogg Slipgate. The GDF must recover this DataDisc and get it to the Transmitter for the awaiting GDF scientists.

Objectives:

  1. The first Island Objective is completed when a GDF Engineer constructs the         generator to lower the security doors.
  2. Once the security doors are lowered, a GDF Soldier must blow the vault doors         with an HE Charge.
  3. The third objective is acquiring the data disk from behind the blown vault doors.         Any GDF unit should take it from the vault and deliver it to the Data Uplink Tower.


Optional Objectives:

  1. Capture the Bunker Spawn Point
  2. Destroy the Bunker Hatch
  3. Capture the Outpost Spawn Point
  4. Construct the Guard Tower
