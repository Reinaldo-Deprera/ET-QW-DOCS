Outskirts

Location: New Jersey, USA

Background: The Strogg have deployed a high-ranking DataBrain to oversee the construction of a new major facility. The GDF must crack the Strogg defenses, steal the DataBrain and take it to the transmitter for analysis by GDF High Command.

Objectives:

  1. A GDF Soldier must destroy a barricade with an HE charge to allow passage for         the MCP.
  2. Any GDF unit must drive the MCP to the Central Outpost
  3. The third objective requires a GDF Covert Ops to hack the Strogg Shield         Generator
  4. The fourth objective: Any GDF unit should steal the Databrain and take it to the         transmitter to upload to GDF H.Q.


Optional Objectives:

  1. Capture the Factory Offices Spawn Point
  2. Capture the Hilltop Building Spawn Point
