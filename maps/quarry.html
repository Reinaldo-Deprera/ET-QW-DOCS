Quarry

Location: Lake District, England

Background: The GDF have forced the Strogg ship to crash-land in a Quarry. The Ship’s auto-destruct has failed, and the Strogg must power it up with three energy cells to prevent the GDF from learning the secrets of deep space travel.

Objectives:

  1. A Strogg Aggressor must destroy the GDF's Jamming Tower generator with a         plasma charge.
  2. The Strogg need to restore power to the crash site by collecting the three         scattered parts and returning them to the crash site. The part locations are         marked on the command map.


Optional Objective:

  1. Capture the Railyard Spawn Point
