Bots

ETQW lets you play against computer-controlled players ("Bots") which help you polish your skills, learn the maps, and fill up servers.

Players joining a Bot server will replace Bots.

To play ETQW by yourself with bots, select ‘Play Computer’ from the main menu and then select your game preferences from the displayed options.

The ETQW bot is designed to give the player a fun and challenging opponent in both offline and online LAN or server environment. The ETQW bot will play alongside the player, helping them to complete the objectives, while enemy bots do their best to foil the player and his team. The ETQW bot will use all of the game’s weapons, vehicles, items, powerups and abilities to help their team win. The ETQW bot is customizable, allowing you to craft the gameplay to whatever suits you best.
