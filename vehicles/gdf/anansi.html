Anansi Attack ’Copter

Crew: 2 Humans
Positions: Pilot, Gunner
Armament: Anti-Missile Decoys, Belly-Mounted Minigun, LAW Missiles, Rockets
Role: Close Air Support
Armor: Light
Speed: Very Fast



Description: The Anansi Attack Copter combines air superiority over the battlefield, and close air support for GDF forces on the ground along with Vertical Take-Off & Landing (VTOL) capability, which eliminates the need for runways. Fast and maneuverable, the Anansi has an internal engine Boost [Shift Key Default] for emergencies. The Pilot has lethal firepower at his command including LAW Guided Missiles, and Rocket Pods, to Anti-Missile Decoys [1 Key Default] for defense. The Gunner can strafe ground and aerial units with the belly-mounted Minigun which has full 360 degree coverage.


The default controls for driving and flying are set with a digital stabilisation. It doesn't change the control scheme, but does make basic control of vehicles easier. To make flying easier for novice pilots, the air vehicle will automatically level itself, making it less crash-prone but harder to maneuver to its maximum extent.

Advanced Driving/Flying (option in shell)

This removes self-levelling default controls, and allows more radical exploration.
