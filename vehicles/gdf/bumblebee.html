Bumblebee Hover Transport

Crew: 5 Humans
Positions: Pilot, 2 Gunners, 2 Passengers
Armament: Anti-Missile Decoys, Mounted GPMG Machine-Gun, 2 Miniguns
Role: Aerial Transport
Armor: Medium
Speed: Medium



Description: When GDF commanders need to insert a squad behind enemy lines in a hurry, they turn to the reliable Bumblebee. With room for up to five GDF troops, the Bumblebee has enough armour to withstand small arms fire from the ground and Anti-Missile Decoys [1 Key Default] for defense against air-to-air, and ground-to-air guided munitions. The Pilot can help soften up hot LZ’s with the nose-mounted GPMG Machine-Gun. The Bumblebee also has respectable firepower when fully manned. Both the starboard, and port side Gunners have mounted Mini-Guns that are perfect for shredding enemy infantry and light vehicles. The two remaining seats allow their Passengers to fire their personal weapons out the sides of the aircraft.


The default controls for driving and flying are set with a digital stabilisation. It doesn't change the control scheme, but does make basic control of vehicles easier. To make flying easier for novice pilots, the air vehicle will automatically level itself, making it less crash-prone but harder to manoeuvre to its maximum extent.

Advanced Driving/Flying (option in shell)

This removes self-levelling default controls, and allows more radical exploration.
