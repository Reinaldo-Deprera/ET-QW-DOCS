Husky Quad

Crew: 1
Positions: Driver
Armament: Anti-Missile Decoys
Role: All-Terrain Personal Transport
Armor: None
Speed: Fast




Description: The unarmed and lightly armoured Husky provides GDF troops with unsurpassed speed and mobility on the battlefield. Able to traverse rugged terrain, and shallow water, the Husky features a Boost [Shift Key Default] for emergency speed, a Handbrake [Left Ctrl Key Default] for tight turns, and Anti-Missile Decoys [1 Key Default] for defense. These all-purpose transports can even be dropped behind enemy lines by parachute to aid stranded troops (requires Vehicle Proficiency Award).
