Titan Tank

Crew: 2 Humans
Positions: Driver, Passenger
Armament: Anti-Missile Decoys, Tank Gun, Minigun
Role: Anti-Armor
Armor: Heavy
Speed: Medium



Description: The Titan main battle tank is the GDF’s primary ground assault vehicle. It is designed to go toe-to-toe with the heaviest armoured combat vehicles, and base defenses, and emerge victorious. All that armor comes at a price however. The Titan is by no means fast, although an internal engine Boost [Shift Key Default] gives it some speed in emergencies. The Driver commands the massive 150mm smooth bore Tank Gun which has incredible power and range, as well as Anti-Missile Decoys [1 Key Default] for defense. The Passenger controls the hatch-mounted Minigun with high elevation tracking for effective anti-aircraft deterrence. The wide tracks make steep terrain or obstacles like wrecked cars no problem for the Titan. However the Titan commander would do well to steer clear of water.
