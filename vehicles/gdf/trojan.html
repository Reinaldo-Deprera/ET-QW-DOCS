Trojan Amphibious Personnel Carrier

Crew: 5 Humans
Positions: Driver, Gunner, 3 Passengers
Armament: Anti-Missile Decoys, Machine Gun, Anti-Aircraft Missiles
Role: Amphibious Armoured Transport
Armor: Medium
Speed: Medium

Description: The Trojan Amphibious Personnel Carrier is the most versatile vehicle on the battlefield. Its heavier armour protects its crew of five from all but the heaviest weapons while its six wheels and aft-mounted waterjets allow it to traverse rough terrain, and even cross deep water. An internal engine Boost [Shift Key Default] gives it respectable speed in emergencies. The Trojan is also designed with roof mounted weapons which do not expose the crew to hostile fire. The driver can direct Machine-Gun fire against targets in the forward quarter, and deploy Anti-Missile Decoys [1 Key Default] for defense. The gunner can engage Strogg air assets in a full 360 degree arc with lethal guided Anti-Aircraft Missiles, which also do respectable damage to Deployables and light vehicles.
