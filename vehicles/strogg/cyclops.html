Cyclops Heavy Walker

Crew: 1 Strogg
Positions: Driver
Armament: Anti-Missile Decoys, Dual-Plasma Cannons, Stomp
Role: Heavy Assault
Armor: Heavy
Speed: Medium



Description: You feel it coming before you ever see it. Thunderous footfalls shake the earth like a harbinger of doom. The most heavily armed and armoured vehicle on or off the planet. The Cyclops Driver charges into the most heavily defended GDF base, knowing his Strogg allies are close behind, obliterating defenses with dual Plasma Cannons, deploying Anti-Missile Decoys [1 Key Default] for defense and the ability to Stomp any infantry beneath him [Spacebar Key Default].


The Strogg Cyclops can crouch to maximize firepower at the expense of mobility. Hit Crouch to go into hunkered-down Siege Mode, and Jump to return to normal.
