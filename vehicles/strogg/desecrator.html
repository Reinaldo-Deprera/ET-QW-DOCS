Desecrator HoverTank

Crew: 2 Strogg
Positions: Driver, Gunner
Armament: Anti-Missile Decoys, Plasma Cannon, Hyperblaster
Role: Anti-Armor
Armor: Medium
Speed: Medium



Description: The low menacing hum of the Desecrator’s gravitic repulsors strikes fear into even the most battle hardened veteran. Though ponderous, the heavily armed and armoured Desecrator is a floating weapons platform, able to glide across land and water alike. The Desecrator is also able to cut power to its repulsors and hunker down into Siege Mode [C Key Default], which increases its defense at the expense of mobility. The driver controls the powerful Plasma Cannon, as well as Anti-Missile Decoys [1 Key Default] for defense. The Gunner can cut down infantry with the roof-mounted Hyperblaster, as well as provide some anti-aircraft defense.
