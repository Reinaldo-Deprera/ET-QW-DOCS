Hog Scout Vehicle

Crew: 2 Strogg
Positions: Driver, Gunner
Armament: Anti-Missile Decoys, Ramming Shield, Mounted Hyperblaster.
Role: Armed Reconnaissance
Armor: Light
Speed: Fast



Description: With a face only an Engineer could love, the Hog (like most Strogg vehicles) makes no concessions to aesthetics or comfort. Its lack of shock absorbers makes handling on rough terrain difficult, but its internal Engine Boost [Shift Key Default] allows it to tear along roads and level terrain at a very high rate of speed. When the Boost is engaged a force field is generated in front of the vehicle allowing it to ram GDF troops and vehicles with devastating effect. The Driver can also deploy Anti-Missile Decoys [1 Key Default] for defence. The Gunner can strafe infantry with the roof-mounted Hyperblaster, and even do respectable damage to low-flying aircraft and other lightly armoured targets.
