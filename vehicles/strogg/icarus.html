Icarus GravPack

Crew: 1 Strogg
Positions: Pilot
Armament: Anti-Missile Decoys, Stroybomb
Role: Personal Transport
Armor: None
Speed: Very Fast




Description: The Icarus GravPack offers Strogg infantry unparalleled freedom of movement on the battlefield. Water, steep terrain, or tall buildings are no obstacle to the Icarus. The gravitic repulsors use a lot of energy, and can only Boost [Shift Key Default] for a short time before they must briefly recharge. So they act more like “Jump-Jets” than “Rocket-Packs”. Able to deploy Anti-Missile Decoys [1 Key Default] for defense, and drop small grenade-like Stroybombs on unwary infantry, Icarus equipped Strogg troopers are a significant challenge to GDF defenders. These versatile transports can even be dropped from orbit on the battlefield to aid stranded troops (requires Vehicle Proficiency Award).
