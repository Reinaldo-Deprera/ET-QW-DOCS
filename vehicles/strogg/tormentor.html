Tormentor Attack Craft

Crew: 2 Strogg
Positions: Pilot, Gunner
Armament: Anti-Missile Decoys, Hyperblaster, Strockets, Plasma Cannon
Role: Air Superiority & Close Air Support
Armor: Light
Speed: Very Fast



Description: The insect-like Tormentor is a familiar sight in the skies above the frontlines. Held aloft by anti-gravity repulsors and even stranger alien technologies, the Tormentor is a feared and highly maneuverable weapons platform. Strogg pilots "plug" into the bio-mechanical aircraft which literally becomes an extension of their will. This allows the Pilot to rain Plasma Cannon and Strocket fire down on GDF targets with great accuracy and effect, as well as deploy Anti-Missile Decoys [1 Key Default] for defense. The Gunner can strafe ground and air targets with the Hyperblaster.


The default controls for driving and flying are set with a digital stabilisation. It doesn't change the control scheme, but does make basic control of vehicles easier. To make flying easier for novice pilots, the air vehicle will automatically level itself, making it less crash-prone but harder to maneuver to its maximum extent.

Advanced Driving/Flying (option in shell)

This removes self-levelling default controls, and allows more radical exploration.
